I'm a Ph.D. student at Université Paris-Saclay in the Probabilités et Statistiques Team and Inria Saclay in the DataShape Team, under supervision of Frédéric Chazal and Pascal Massart.

My research focuses on Topological Data Analysis (TDA), a recent field which aims at extracting and exploiting topological information out of complex and possibly high dimensional data. More precisely, I work toward developing statistically well-founded and implementable TDA methods to analyse graph structured data.
