Ph.D. Student

My research focuses on Topological Data Analysis (TDA), a recent field that aims at extracting and exploiting topological information out of complex and possibly high dimensional data. More precisely, I work toward developing statistically well-founded and implementable TDA methods to analyse graph structured data.
